The Scalable Technology Enablement Program (STEP) is Raptor Aero’s end-to-end support system for drone OEMs. It delivers a complete, production-ready UAV stack tailored to your mission needs — significantly accelerating your product development.

STEP is ideal for:

  • Drone startups
  • System integrators
  • OEMs entering new drone verticals
  • Component companies looking to deliver drone platforms under their brand

    • You receive: •Aerodynamically tuned airframe •Motors, ESCs, Propellers •Battery + Smart BMS + Power Distribution •Flight controller, GPS, RTK, Gimbals, Sensors •RaptorOS (PX4-based), Raptor GCS, SkySight tools •Assembly, calibration, and documentation •5-year spare support and optional services
